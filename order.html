Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept various file formats including PDF, AI, PSD, INDD, JPG, PNG, and TIFF. For the best print quality, we recommend vector formats (PDF, AI) or high-resolution (300 DPI) raster images.

How long does it take to complete an order?

Production times vary depending on the type of product, quantity, and current order volume. Standard business cards typically take 3-5 business days, while more complex projects may take 7-10 business days. Rush services are available for most products at an additional cost.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we offer professional design services for all our print products. Our experienced designers can create custom designs or make adjustments to your existing files. Design services are priced separately based on the complexity and requirements of your project.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For corporate clients, we also offer net-30 payment terms upon approval of credit application.

Do you offer shipping or delivery?

Yes, we offer shipping through reliable carriers to any location. Local delivery is available for orders within a 30-mile radius of our facility. Shipping and delivery costs are calculated based on weight, dimensions, and destination.
