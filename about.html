Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders

5+ Years Excellence

Universal Printing Press was founded in 2019 by Dr. Daniel Adu, who had a vision to create a printing company that would combine traditional printing techniques with emerging digital technologies.

Starting with the aim of promoting education and marketing through printing services, we've grown into a full-service printing facility with state-of-the-art equipment and a team of skilled professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional quality on every project.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients, embracing new technologies while maintaining our commitment to craftsmanship and attention to detail that has been our hallmark since day one.

Today, Universal Printing Press is proud to serve a diverse range of clients, from small local businesses to large corporations, providing comprehensive printing solutions that help them communicate effectively with their audiences.
