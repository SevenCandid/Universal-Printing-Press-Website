Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept a wide range of file formats including PDF, AI, PSD, INDD, JPG, PNG, and TIFF. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDF files with fonts embedded and images at 300 DPI or higher.

How long does it take to complete a printing job?

The turnaround time depends on the complexity and size of your project. Standard business cards usually take 2-3 business days, while larger projects like catalogs may take 7-10 business days. We also offer rush services for an additional fee.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we have a team of professional designers who can help create or improve your designs. Whether you need a complete design from scratch or just some tweaks to your existing artwork, we can assist you at competitive rates.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product. Business cards start at 100 pieces, while flyers and brochures typically start at 250 pieces. For custom projects, please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Do you offer shipping services?

Yes, we offer shipping services nationwide. Shipping costs are calculated based on weight, dimensions, and delivery location. We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your prints arrive safely and on time.
